My favorite restaurant is The Jazz

I really like eating at this restaurant because they have really good seafood pastas. Sometimes they will have live music there. One time they had a one man band that was really cool.

What can you eat?

  1. 1/2 lb of Crawfish
  2. Spicy Crab Pasta
  3. Oysters
